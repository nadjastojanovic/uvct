Our
Vision

Impact

UVCT has a motto of finding ways and means to transform the world’s burden of population into assets of human resource through a process of empowerment, rather than of control. We bring into the mainstream drop–outs, under achievers and children in conflict with the law, especially those belonging to marginalized and deprived sections of society, and help them become socially responsible and economically productive citizens of India and the world.

Our Objectives

awareness

Develop awareness through seminars, group discussions and camps to help young adults find solutions to their problems- psychological, financial, ethical and social.

realistic valve system

Help acquire a realistic valve system that is conducive to the development of a healthy society and to develop the concept of oneness of all religions and love for the homeland in the youth, by raising their level of consciousness.

education & skills

Develop educational and skill training programs for school drop-outs and under- achievers, to enable them to become not only economically productive but also socially responsible citizens of India and the world.

counselling centres

Develop counseling centres for the disturbed youth; and to establish rehabilitation centre’s for juvenile delinquents to provide both emotional support and skill training.

industry skills

Develop skills among the young adults and women, in regard to textiles, handicrafts and other ancillary industries.

equality

Endeavor to promote equality of status and opportunity, assuring dignity of individual and the unity and integrity of the Nation, services for the hygiene, health and happiness for all the citizens of India.

financial literacy

Provide Financial Literacy for youth and women in backward communities for employment and formal banking related awareness.

Urivi Vikram Charitable Trust (UVCT) is the organization which successfully drew the attention of the Nation to the urgency for adolescent-centric programmes and built the first ever National Centre for Adolescents.
