UVCT

Urivi Vikram Charitable Trust is a national level non-governmental organization, with focus on the social and economic empowerment of young adults since 1991.

  • Vision
  • About Us
  • Gallery
  • Contact

The Trust rightly visualizes that due care must be given to the stage of adolescence, a period of rapid development and identity crisis in one’s growth. It has also been undertaking projects for women empowerment and conducting awareness campaigns on public health, environmental protection and reformation/rehabilitation of delinquents.

Projects

Prerana

Started in July 1993, the project is providing career guidance and psychological counseling to school/college going children.

Shakti

The project provides financial assistance in the form of school/college fees, books & stationery and other required support to those students who are academically brilliant, but economically poor.

Sahara

The project provides vocational training in various marketable skills at its Centers and helps the participants with linkages to livelihood.

Ummeed

The project provides vocational training in various marketable skills at its Centers and helps the participants with linkages to livelihood.

Parivartan

Started in March 2007, the project aims to bring the delinquent children into the mainstream through ‘Life Skills Education’ education and skill up-gradation.
